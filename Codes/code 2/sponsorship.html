Sponsorship Packages

Supporting a young driver at the beginning of their journey puts your brand in a story that grows. Every package comes with real, trackable deliverables — not vague promises. Choose the level that fits your goals.

Entry level

Supporter

Good for: local businesses and individuals

  • Logo on KSM Racing website
  • Thank-you post on social media
  • Monthly progress mention with your brand tagged
  • Name in race event captions

Mid-tier

Race Partner

Good for: small brands and growing businesses

  • Logo on website and race content photos
  • 2 dedicated social posts per month featuring your brand
  • Race event updates sent to you after each event
  • Professional karting photography for your use
  • Instagram story coverage from race days

Top tier

Main Partner

Good for: brands wanting consistent, visible exposure

  • Featured placement on website homepage
  • Logo on clothing and karting content
  • Regular content across Instagram and TikTok
  • Full race reports with brand mentions after every event
  • Dedicated sponsor story post
  • Priority photography access — images cleared for your marketing

What this actually looks like

Professional photography

A photographer is on site at most race events. You receive high-resolution images cleared for use in your own social media, website or advertising.

Social media reach

Posts go out on Instagram and TikTok tied to real race days, not generic content. Your logo or brand name appears in caption, image or both.

Race reports

After every race, a short written update covers results, key moments and progress. Race Partner and Main Partner sponsors are mentioned by name.
