# Project Overview

## Purpose of the website
The website introduces Kseniia Miliaeva as a young karting driver. It tells her
story, shows her racing experience and presents her in a clear, professional way
online. It is also a learning project for practising HTML and CSS.

## Who the website is for
It is for Kseniia (KSM Racing) to use as her own racing profile that she can share
with other people.

## Target audience
- **The racing community** – other drivers, teams and people involved in karting.
- **Followers** – people who follow her on Instagram and TikTok and want to learn
  more about her.
- **Possible sponsors** – people or brands who might want to support a young driver
  and get visibility in racing content.

## Main sections of the website
1. **Hero / Intro** – name, short tagline and a main photo.
2. **About Me / My Story** – how she started karting and what she is like.
3. **Driver Profile** – racing style, level and experience.
4. **Tracks** – the tracks she races at and what she learns at each one.
5. **Gallery** – racing photos.
6. **Goals** – what she wants to achieve next.
7. **Sponsors** – information for sponsors and the racing community.
8. **Contact** – a contact form and email button for teams and sponsors.
9. **Footer** – copyright line.
