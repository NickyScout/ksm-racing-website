Partner with KSM Racing

I am at the beginning of my racing journey and I am looking to build real relationships with local businesses who would like to support a young driver as she grows.

This is not just about putting a logo on a website. I want to create useful, positive content for my supporters through race-day photos, short videos, progress updates and branded posts that local businesses can actually share with their own audience.

Early partners can be featured through:

  • Logo placement on my website
  • Logo on race gear or teamwear where event rules allow
  • Branded karting photos and short videos
  • Mentions in race-day updates and race reports
  • Monthly progress posts you can share with your own audience
  • Local business shout-outs across Instagram and TikTok
  • Race reports and sponsor thank-you posts

I am especially interested in working with local businesses who want to support youth sport, women in motorsport, driver development and a positive racing story from the very beginning.

Partnership options

Getting started

Starter Supporter

Good for a local business that wants to help you get started.

  • Logo on website sponsor section
  • Thank-you post on Instagram, TikTok and LinkedIn
  • Mention in race updates throughout the season
  • Logo in selected race-day photos or graphics
  • Monthly progress update you can repost

Recommended

Gear Partner

Probably the strongest early option — your brand goes wherever I go.

  • Logo on race hoodie, cap, training top or teamwear
  • Logo on website
  • Thank-you posts during race weekends
  • Branded photos from karting sessions
  • Short video or reel featuring your brand
  • Permission to use selected photos for your own social media

Per event

Race Day Partner

For a business that wants to support one specific event.

  • "Race day supported by [your business]" across all posts
  • Logo on race-day social graphics
  • Mention before and after the event
  • 3–5 usable photos from the day
  • Short race report mentioning your support

Founding partner

Founding Partner

For someone who believes in the journey early — and grows alongside it.

  • Larger logo placement on website
  • Logo on race gear where event rules allow
  • Regular mentions across all updates
  • Branded content throughout the season
  • A dedicated "partner story" post about why you supported me
  • First option to continue as my audience grows
A note on gear and kart logos: As a rental driver I do not always control the kart livery. All logo placement is described as: "Logo placement on race gear, teamwear and selected media content where event rules allow." This keeps everything accurate and professional.
